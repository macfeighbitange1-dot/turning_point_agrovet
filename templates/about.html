About Turning Point Agrovet

The Science of Farming, The Heart of Kirinyaga.

Our Mission

At Turning Point Agrovet, we believe that modern farming is the backbone of Kenya's economy. Our mission is to empower farmers in Kerugoya, Mwea, Kutus, and beyond by providing access to high-quality seeds, fertilizers, and science-backed agricultural consultancy.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expertise: Our team consists of seasoned agronomists who understand the specific soil and climate of Kirinyaga County.
  • Quality Assurance: We only stock certified products from trusted global and local brands.
  • Community First: We aren't just a shop; we are your partners in the field, from planting to harvest.
