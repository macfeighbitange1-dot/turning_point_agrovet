Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver to Mwea or Sagana?

Yes! We offer coordinated delivery services across Kirinyaga County. Contact our support line after placing an order to arrange transport.

Can I get a farm visit from an agronomist?

Absolutely. You can request a professional consultancy through our "Consultancy" page. We provide on-site soil testing and crop health assessments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We primarily accept M-Pesa for online orders and cash or M-Pesa at our physical store in Kerugoya Town.
